Managing Personal Preferences


You can choose various preferences to increase your work efficiency.
The preferences will be saved to your computer's local profile.

Follow the instructions below to modify your IDE preferences.
1. Open the preferences window by choosing Tools > Preferences.
2. Modify your preferences.
3. Click OK to save them.

You can restore the default preferences whenever necessary.

You can also change your window management by choosing the Window menu.
Those window management options can be reset to default from the same menu.
